About & disclaimer

Not for navigation. This map is an informational overlay built from volunteer-contributed OpenStreetMap data. Coverage is incomplete and may be out of date — especially for snow-chain rules, seasonal restrictions, and newly built roads. Always verify with local signage and official sources.

Data updates: the OSM snapshot is rebuilt manually — currently about once a month. The date below is the snapshot this page is using.

Coverage: the dashed outline on the map shows the exact area of the source extract (Geofabrik “europe”). Anything inside the outline is processed; outside it the map has no data even where roads are visible on the basemap.
